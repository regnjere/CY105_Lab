  1. Headers

    Insert the headers (image below) through the small holes on the front of the PCB. Make sure the connector is flush with the surface of the PCB.

  2. Solder the headers

    While holding the headers in place, flip the board over and solder all the connector pins. DO NOT solder on the front of the board.

  3. Attach the breadboard

    Flip the board back over to the side that says “front.” Peel off the cover on the back of the breadboard and attach it below the headers as shown in the image below.

  4. Bolt the Bluefruit and the display

    Attach the display (TFT Gizmo) to the front (image below/left) and the Bluefruit to the back (image below/right) of the circuit board using the 12 provided screws. Note that the TFT Gizmo label on the display and the USB connector on the Bluefruit should be aligned towards the top of the board. You'll have to remove the orange screw covers on the display. Be gentle when using the screws to attach the display. It is fragile!

  5. Stand

    Attach the circuit board to the acrylic stand.

  6. Instructor Check

    Verify with your instructor that you have correctly assembled the kit!

  7. Stand

    Attach the circuit board to the acrylic stand.
